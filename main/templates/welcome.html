gae-init is the easiest way to kick start new web applications on Google App Engine.

Check it in action with live demos and get started:

gae-init gae-init-babel gae-init-upload gae-init-debug

The documentation is not complete! But you are welcome to contribute or send feedback with your questions.

Features

  • Multiple OAuth options to sign-in
  • Manage, delete or merge signed in users
  • Secret keys and other settings are stored securely in Datastore
  • Working feedback form
  • Custom pages for error handlings, like 404
  • SEO friendly with stuff like sitemaps.xml, robots.txt and even favicon.ico
  • Much more...
